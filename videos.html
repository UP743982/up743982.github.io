Side Note:

Most of words you encounter for the first time won't stay in your Active vocabulary. Instead, they will gradually build up in your Pasive vocabulary where you will be able to recall them during conversation. To learn new language, one must first saturate Passive vocabulary with new words constatntly. Watching videos is a good way to improve Passive vocabulary!
