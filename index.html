About the Tool

It is known that 3000 words make up about 95% of English language needed to maintain everyday conversation. It does apply to most languages!

There are two different vocabularies that you need to consider: Passive and Active.

Passive Vocabulary consist of words that you recognise and understand, however don't use them in your conversations.
Active Vocabulary consist of all the words you understand and use in daily life conversations.

New words will first get absorbed into learner's Passive vocabulary as see them for the first time. With enough repetition, those words will move into learner's Active vocabulary.

It is believed that 250-300 words is enough to be able to start having simple everyday conversations. Having 1000-3000 words in your memory pocket would make you able to survive most any conversation!

Main idea of the teaching tool is to provide learner with enough help to improve their Active vocabulary and help start using Spanish language in everyday conversations.
